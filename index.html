What to do

Trekking

Thinking of Trekking in Kerala? Well, this will be one of the best decisions of your life as this place showcases spectacular vistas and owns a vast bucket of trekking trails

Snorkelling

Snorkeling in Kovalam is an activity which is best experienced during the months when the sea is calm; these months are from December to March.

Kayaking

Kayaking in Kerala "God's Own Country" is an outstanding way to enjoy adventure sports. Kerala is known for adventures of assorted types; be it on land or water.

Houseboating

Have you ever gone cruising in a houseboat on the backwaters of Kerala? If you haven't, make sure you do.
